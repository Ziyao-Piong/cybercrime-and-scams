Think Before You Click!

Don't blindly click on any link you come across online!

Phishing scams often trick users by sending fake emails or messages with malicious links. Before you click, it's important to stop and think about where that link might take you. Clicking a bad link could lead to malware, theft of your personal information, or worse!

Learn more about the different types of phishing scams to better protect yourself. Below are some common types of phishing attacks you should be aware of:

  • Business Email Compromise (BEC) Phishing
  • Clone Phishing
  • Pop-up Phishing
  • Search Engine Phishing
  • Smishing (SMS Phishing)
  • Spear Phishing
  • Voice Phishing (Vishing)
