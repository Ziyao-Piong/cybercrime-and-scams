Can you spot the phishing scams?

Phishing Awareness Tips

Identifying phishing scams is crucial in today's digital world. By staying vigilant and knowing what to look for, you can protect yourself from falling victim to these scams.

  • Check the sender's email address: Look for misspellings or suspicious domains.
  • Be wary of urgent or threatening language: Scammers often create a false sense of urgency.
  • Hover over links before clicking: Verify that the URL matches the legitimate website.
  • Look for generic greetings: Phishing emails often use "Dear Sir/Madam" instead of your name.
  • Be cautious of requests for sensitive information: Legitimate organisations rarely ask for personal data via email.
